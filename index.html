ABOUT ME

Non Sibi Sed Omnibus
(Not for self but for others)

Born and Raised in West Africa Sierra Leone, Dijah is the first of four children in her nuclear family. She started out her work at a very young age as a human right's activist, raising awareness amongst young adults. When given the opportunity to travel to the US, she understood that necessity was laid upon her to do her best, at that very moment she had become a symbol of hope, not only to her family but also the young adults she's worked with.

She attended Morgan State University, majoring in Economics. During an internship, she realized that she was not interested in the field and decided to take some time of to consider her options, this gave her much time to reflect and when presented with the opportunity to become a software engineer, this time she carefully researched and realized this is exactly what she wanted to do.

SYMPHINITY

Created a web app that allows user to search up songs and receive suggestions based on their search history. Users are also able to add songs to their favorite list, as well edit said list.

This app was built using, React Redux and Nodejs, the app requires users to register and login after which they can access their account, users can search up songs and will be able to retrieve the song and suggested songs based on the name of their searched song, if available in the database, and add to their favorite list.
